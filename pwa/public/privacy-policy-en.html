Privacy Policy

App Introduction

NonBlockingLife is a task management application designed to help users focus on their life's main thread, embodying the principle of "Don't Block Your Life's Main Thread."

1. How We Handle Your Data

NonBlockingLife does not collect, store, or transmit any user personal data.

  • All your tasks, preferences, and application data are stored exclusively on your device (local storage)
  • The app supports offline mode and works without internet connection
  • We do not collect, access, or view any of your data
  • We have no knowledge of your identity or how you use the app

2. Local Data Storage

All application data (task lists, settings, preferences, etc.) is stored locally on your device using:

  • Browser LocalStorage
  • IndexedDB

This data never leaves your device unless you actively choose to export or synchronize it.

3. Optional Google Sheets Synchronization

If you choose to synchronize your data across multiple devices, we offer optional integration with Google Sheets. Importantly:

  • This feature is completely optional - you can use the app without it
  • You must set up your own Google Apps Script using your own Google account
  • The NonBlockingLife app will never know, store, or collect your Google account information
  • The app only connects to the Google Apps Script execution URL that you provide (which you deploy yourself)
  • All data exchanges occur directly between you and your Google Sheets - we cannot see any of it
  • You have complete control over the Google Apps Script and can disable or delete it at any time

4. System Notifications

NonBlockingLife uses system notifications for the following reasons:

  • To remind you of your current task status (inspired by the Pomodoro Technique)
  • Notifications run locally on your device
  • Notification content is not sent to any remote server
  • You can enable or disable notifications in the app settings

5. Third-Party Services

NonBlockingLife does not use:

  • Google Analytics or any other analytics tools
  • Firebase or other remote Google services
  • Advertising networks or tracking systems
  • Any other third-party data collection services

6. External Content Links

The app may contain links to external websites (such as YouTube tutorials, Superconductor-like Society webpage, etc.). Please note:

  • These external websites are managed by their respective owners and are not covered by our privacy policy
  • Please review the privacy policy of any external website before accessing it
  • We are not responsible for external website content

7. Login and Authentication

NonBlockingLife does not require login and does not collect any personally identifiable information:

  • No user account required
  • No email address required
  • No name or other personal information required
  • No location or device information collected

8. Data Export and Deletion

Your data is completely under your control:

  • You can export your data at any time
  • You can delete all data in the app at any time
  • All local data will be removed when you uninstall the app
  • No need to send us a deletion request - the data is already in your hands

9. Children's Privacy

NonBlockingLife is not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has used this app, we will take immediate steps to ensure compliance.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Your continued use of the app means you accept the updated privacy policy.
